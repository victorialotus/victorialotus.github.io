The Artist

Elevating moments beyond the ordinary

Victoria Lotus is an aerial arts performer and instructor whose work lives at the intersection of athleticism, artistry, and spiritual presence. Trained in aerial silk and lyra, every performance is a hand-crafted experience designed to captivate.

From intimate gatherings to large-scale events, the Victoria Lotus Performer experience brings wonder, elegance, and breathtaking skill to every stage — leaving audiences transformed long after the curtain falls.
